About

Achieving an honours degree in fitness and personal training allowed me to gain great knowledge on the anatomy of the body and how to train to strengthen our capabilities as human beings. Throughtout my degree I progressively worked at earning my REPs accreditations passing my way up to a level 4 trainer. Through gaining these certifications i piled up invaluable experiences by training a large amount of variety clients.

Alongside my work in personal training we worked on targetting the athletic population. I progressed my knowledge on how the above average human needed catering for in order to keep competing at their highest level. This is where I earned my sports conditioning badge.

Having trained clients of a range of ages and gained so much knowledge and experience the necessity of injury correction and prevention quickly became apparent. So achieving my professional diploma in sort tissue therapy and corrective exercieswas an absolute must in order to provide clients with a means to stay fit or return to fit as quick as possible.

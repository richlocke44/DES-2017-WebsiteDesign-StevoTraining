Welcome to Stevo Training

Personal Training

Personal Training is offered to our clients to provide them with one to one sessions, delivered by a friendly and motivating trainer who cares about your fitness goals. These sessions in an ego free and competition free gym to allow clients to be at ease whilst training to participate maximumly without anxiety.

Sports Conditioning

The Sports Conditioning aspect of training is usually selected by our athlete clientele as the methods and exercises are tailored specifically to enhance performance around an individuals sport. However, anyone looking for a different style of exercise or even to improve recreational sporting performance are always welcome for this section of training.

Sports Masssage

Our Sports Massage is aimed to relieve pain through soft tissue manipulation and tailored around improving function either from simple release strategies or working on restoring an injury back to normal. Corrective exercises are offered and when selected alongside one of our training packages is not only a client favourite but the results are fantastic too!

Sports Massage

Through soft tissue manipulation and release, the sports massage deals with a vast array of sporting injuries and functional problems.

This is done by techniques that break down fibral athesions that cause muscle inefficiencies.

The massage is coupled with corrected exercises offered to maintain improvements made during massage sessions and aim to reain integrity of muscles and joints.
